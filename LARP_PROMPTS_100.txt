========================================================================
DIFFICULTY NOTES (for the game / judge tuning)
========================================================================
- MUNDANE (most of these): the challenge is CREATIVITY — making the
  trivial sound meaningful without inventing fake scale.
- SEMI-REAL (olympiad, TA award, spelling bee, 5K): the challenge is
  RESTRAINT — real-ish achievements that are easy to oversell.
- Want prestige-tier prompts too (e.g. "You won ISEF", "Closed a
  Series A")? Those test restraint hardest and can be a separate
  "hard mode" pool.
